Your workspace is your team's home in VastgoedFotoAI.nl. Customize it to match your brand and workflow preferences.

## Accessing Workspace Settings

1. Click your workspace name in the sidebar
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown
3. Navigate through the settings tabs

## General Settings

### Workspace Name

Your workspace name appears throughout VastgoedFotoAI.nl. Choose something recognizable like your agency or company name.

### Workspace URL

Your custom workspace URL:

```
app.vastgoedfotoai.nl/your-workspace-name
```

This URL is used for sharing and bookmarking.

### Logo

Upload your company logo to personalize your workspace:

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Recommended size | 200×50 pixels |
| Formats | PNG, SVG, or JPG |
| Max file size | 2 MB |

## Default Settings

Configure defaults for new projects:

### Default Style Template

Set which template is pre-selected when creating new projects. Team members can always change this per project.

### Default Export Format

Choose the default download format:
- High Resolution (original quality)
- Web Optimized (compressed)

### Auto-Processing

Enable to automatically start processing after upload. Disable for manual control over each batch.

<div class="callout-tip">

**Recommended:** Keep auto-processing disabled until you're familiar with VastgoedFotoAI.nl. This gives you time to preview templates before using credits.

</div>

## Notifications

Configure how you receive notifications:

- **Email notifications** – Processing complete, low credits, team invites
- **Browser notifications** – Real-time alerts in-app
- **Weekly digest** – Summary of workspace activity

## Data & Privacy

### Data Retention

Configure how long processed images are stored:
- 30 days (default)
- 60 days
- 90 days

### Export Data

Download all your workspace data including projects, settings, and billing history.

### Delete Workspace

<div class="callout-warning">

**Warning:** Deleting your workspace permanently removes all projects, images, and settings. This action cannot be undone.

</div>
